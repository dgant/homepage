This is a demonstration of the game engine that my Gateway Lab team and I prepared for further development through the Columbia Service-Learning Program.

Follow the green arrow to find and collect HIV medications. Avoid the HIV viruses which will attack you, and dodge the bouncy red blood cells. Guide the ship through the bloodstream using the arrow keys:

Up Arrow: Accelerate forward
Down arrow: Accelerate backward
Left/Right Arrows: Rotate
R: Restart

This game was created with Macromedia Director MX and requires your browser to have the Macromedia Shockwave plugin, which you may download here.

Welcome

Bandits! is a four-player custom map for Blizzard Entertainment's WarCraft III: The Frozen Throne. Command a pack of brigands and outlaws in a quest to accumulate great power and wealth. Players fight for control of trade routes across a vast kingdom, while fending off the forces of the vile Sheriff. Learn more.

Features

  • Wild and wooly free-for-all action. Destructive behavior is rewarded (just like in the real world)!
  • A wide variety of underhanded and just-plain-rotten tactics and strategies. Fun for the whole family!
  • A vibrant interactive world you can explore (or burn to the ground).
  • AI opponents for single-player games, filling slots, or to show you how to play.

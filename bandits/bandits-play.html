Feedback

Although it has been released Bandits! is still a work in progress. Here's how you can contribute:

  • Send a replay of any game of Bandits you play to BanditsGame@gmail.com
  • If you observed a bug, describe what you observed, and where/when it could be observed in the replay.
  • If you find that certain units, abilities, or strategies are overpowered, explain what and how.
  • Let me know how you like Bandits! If there's elements of gameplay you do or don't like, let me know.

Project STAY Game Demo (2004)

Follow the green arrow to find and collect HIV medications. Avoid the HIV viruses which will attack you, and dodge the bouncy red blood cells. Guide the ship through the bloodstream using the arrow keys:

  • Up/down arrows: Accelerate forward/backward
  • Left/right arrows: Rotate
  • R: Reset ship

Refresh your browser window to start the demo from scratch.
